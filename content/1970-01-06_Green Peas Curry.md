1. prepare
    - 1 onion
    - 1 can grean peas
    - 1/2 can tomato
2. chop onion and fry until light golden brown
3. put in tomato and fry for 5 mins until bright red becomes orange
4. add
    - garlic powder
    - curry powder
    - chilli powder
    - salt
    - pepper
    - green peas
5. mix and simmer for 5 minutes
